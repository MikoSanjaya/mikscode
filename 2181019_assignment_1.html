What is UNAI?

UNAI is a higher education institution managed by the Seventh-day adventist church of the western union of Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that it was founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleiding school der Advent Zending. In the past, this school was founded for the education of the evangelists of the seventh-day adventist church


UNAI has 6 faculties :

Faculty of IT

Faculty of philosophy

Faculty of Teacher training & education

Faculty of economics

Faculty of Nursing

Faculty of Mathemathics and natural sciences
